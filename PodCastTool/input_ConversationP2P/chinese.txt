
[Title 1] 早起与晨练 - Buổi sáng thức dậy và tập thể dục

F|大卫每天早上七点准时起床。|Dàwèi měitiān zǎoshang qī diǎn zhǔnshí qǐchuáng.|David wakes up on time at seven every morning.
F|他穿上运动服，去操场跑三公里。|Tā chuān shàng yùndòngfú, qù cāochǎng pǎo sān gōnglǐ.|He puts on sportswear and goes to the playground to run three kilometers.
F|清晨的空气很新鲜，让他觉得很有精神。|Qīngchén de kōngqì hěn xīnxiān, ràng tā juédé hěn yǒu jīngshén.|The early morning air is very fresh, making him feel very energetic.
F|跑步以后，他在路边的小摊买了一个热包子。|Pǎobù yǐhòu, tā zài lùbiān de xiǎotān mǎile yīgè rè bāozi.|After running, he bought a hot steamed bun at a roadside stall.
