

[Title 1] 迷失在街头 - Lạc lối giữa phố thị

F|大卫站在繁华的十字路口，看着路标感到迷茫。|Dàwèi zhàn zài fánhuá de shízì lùkǒu, kànzhe lùbiāo gǎndào mímáng.|David stood at a busy intersection, looking at the road signs feeling lost.
F|虽然学了几个月汉语，但他发现还是听不懂路人的对话。|Suīrán xuéle jǐ gè yuè Hànyǔ, dàn tā fāxiàn háishì tīng bù dǒng lùrén de duìhuà.|Although he had studied Chinese for a few months, he found he still couldn't understand the conversations of passersby.
F|那些汉字在他眼里就像一串串解不开的密码。|Nàxiē Hànzì zài tā yǎnlǐ jiù xiàng yī chuànchuàn jiě bù kāi de mìmǎ.|Those Chinese characters looked like strings of unbreakable codes in his eyes.
F|挫败感像冬天的冷风，让他有些不知所措。|Cuòbàigǎn xiàng dōngtiān de lěngfēng, ràng tā yǒuxiē bùzhī suǒcuò.|The sense of frustration was like a cold winter wind, making him feel a bit at a loss.

[Title 2] 公园里的老爷爷 - Ông cụ ở công viên

F|他走进一家安静的公园，看到一位老爷爷在练习太极。|Tā zǒu jìn yījiā ānjìng de gōngyuán, kàndào yī wèi lǎoyéyé zài liànxí tàijí.|He walked into a quiet park and saw an old man practicing Tai Chi.
F|老爷爷笑着对他招手，用缓慢而清晰的汉语和他打招呼。|Lǎoyéyé xiàozhe duì tā zhāoshǒu, yòng huǎnmàn ér qīngxī de Hànyǔ hé tā dǎzhāohū.|The old man smiled and waved at him, greeting him in slow and clear Chinese.
F|大卫试着回答，惊讶地发现自己能听懂大部分的内容。|Dàwèi shìzhe huídá, jīngyà de fāxiàn zìjǐ néng tīng dǒng dà bùfèn de nèiróng.|David tried to answer and was surprised to find he could understand most of the content.
F|那刻，语言不再是课本上的公式，而是温暖的交流。|Nà kè, yǔyán bù zài shì kèběn shàng de gōngshì, ér shì wēnnuǎn de jiāoliú.|At that moment, language was no longer a formula in a textbook, but a warm exchange.

[Title 3] 菜市场的烟火气 - Hơi thở cuộc sống ở chợ dân sinh

F|为了挑战自己，大卫决定去热闹的菜市场买水果。|Wèile tiǎozhàn zìjǐ, Dàwèi juédìng qù rènào de càishìchǎng mǎi shuǐguǒ.|To challenge himself, David decided to go to a bustling wet market to buy fruit.
F|到处都是叫卖声，空气中弥漫着生活的气息。|Dàochù dōu shì jiàomài shēng, kōngqì zhōng mímànzhe shēnghuó de qìxí.|Cries of vendors were everywhere, and the air was filled with the breath of life.
F|他成功地用汉语砍价，还学会了几个地道的词汇。|Tā chénggōng de yòng Hànyǔ kǎnjià, hái xuéhuìle jǐ gè dìdào de cíhuì.|He successfully bargained in Chinese and even learned a few authentic vocabulary words.
F|这次小小的胜利，让他对自己的汉语有了信心。|Zhè cì xiǎoxiǎo de shènglì, ràng tā duì zìjǐ de Hànyǔ yǒule xìnxīn.|This small victory gave him confidence in his Chinese.

[Title 4] 城市天台的思考 - Suy ngẫm trên sân thượng thành phố

F|傍晚，大卫爬上一座大楼的天台，俯瞰整座城市的灯火。|Bàngwǎn, Dàwèi pá shàng yīzuò dàlóu de tiāntái, fǔkàn zhěng zuò chéngshì de dēnghuǒ.|In the evening, David climbed to the rooftop of a building, overlooking the lights of the entire city.
F|他戴着耳机，听着一首中文流行歌曲，竟然听懂了歌词。|Tā dàizhe ěrjī, tīngzhe yī shǒu Zhōngwén liúxíng gēqǔ, jìngrán tīng dǒngle gēcí.|He wore headphones, listening to a Chinese pop song, and surprisingly understood the lyrics.
F|那些旋律似乎在讲述着他这段时间的心路历程。|Nàxiē xuánlǜ sìhū zài jiǎngshùzhe tā zhè duàn shíjiān de xīnlù lìchéng.|Those melodies seemed to be telling the story of his journey during this time.
F|他意识到，语言的精髓在于感受，而不仅仅是记忆。|Tā yìshí dào, yǔyán de jīngsuǐ zàiyú gǎnshòu, ér bù jǐnjǐn shì jìyì.|He realized that the essence of language lies in feeling, not just memory.

[Title 5] 跨越边界的信 - Lá thư vượt qua biên giới

F|回到住处，他没有打开电脑，而是拿出一张明信片。|Huídào zhùchù, tā méiyǒu dǎkāi diànnǎo, ér shì ná chū yī zhāng míngxìnpiàn.|Returning to his place, he didn't turn on the computer, but took out a postcard.
F|他用端正的汉字给中国朋友写了一段感谢的话。|Tā yòng duānzhèng de Hànzì gěi Zhōngguó péngyǒu xiěle yīduàn gǎnxiè de huà.|He wrote a message of thanks to a Chinese friend in neat Chinese characters.
F|虽然写得慢，但每一个字都充满了真实的感情。|Suīrán xiě dé màn, dàn měi yīgè zì dōu chōngmǎnle zhēnshí de gǎnqíng.|Although he wrote slowly, every character was full of genuine emotion.
F|大卫知道，他的汉语旅程才刚刚开始，但他已不再恐惧。|Dàwèi zhīdào, tā de Hànyǔ lǚchéng cái gānggāng kāishǐ, dàn tā yǐ bù zài kǒngjù.|David knew his Chinese journey had just begun, but he was no longer afraid.
